Best Series According to Susanna

My Top 3 Series of all time

1. F.R.I.E.N.D.S

This series has my heart. It is very connecting, funny, lovable and very enjoyable and my favourite character is Joey.

2. Money Heist

I love the unpredictable nature of this series. Love the attachment between the characters as the series progresses. It is very enjoyable to watch the heist conducted by a group.

3. XO Kitty

The series is very cute and depicts the teenagers' life.
It also shows the life of a student in another country.
